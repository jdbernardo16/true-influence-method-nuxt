## STEP 8 — SESSIONS

**Session structure overview (to share with client):**

---

**Beginning Private Training (4 sessions)**

Session 1 — The Discovery Interview
Joanna leads a deep interview to surface your story, your defining moment, and the truth behind your work. No preparation needed. Just honesty.

Session 2 — Your Message
From the discovery material, we build your core message — a clear, resonant articulation of who you are and what you stand for.

Session 3 — Your Voice
We explore how you speak — your natural strengths, your blocks, your presence. We begin developing the voice that carries your message.

Session 4 — Your Story in the World
We bring it all together. You practice expressing your message with clarity and confidence. You leave with a foundation that is entirely yours.

---

**Intermediate Private Training (10 sessions = 4 above + 6 below)**

Sessions 5–10 — Signature Talk Development
We build your signature talk from the ground up — structure, narrative arc, emotional resonance, and delivery. By Session 10, you have a talk you can take anywhere.

---

**Advanced Private Training (14 sessions = 10 above + 4 below)**

Sessions 11–14 — Delivery & Thought Leadership
Full delivery training — voice, body, presence, and command. You step into your role as a thought leader, fully equipped to speak in any room with authority and impact.

---

**Between every session:**

-   Session recap notes sent by Joanna’s team within 48 hours
-   Practice prompts or reflection exercises where relevant
-   Open email access to Joanna’s team for questions

**Quarterly Mastermind & Retreat:**
All private clients are invited to Joanna’s quarterly retreat experience during their 90-day engagement.
Details sent separately once onboarding is complete.
