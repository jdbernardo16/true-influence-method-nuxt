# PAGE 3: THE JOURNEY

## HERO

**Tag:** The Process

**Headline:**
A Process Rooted in Truth. Built Around You.

**Body:**
The True Influence Method™️ is not a framework you slot yourself into. It’s a living process — responsive, deep, and entirely grounded in who you are. It moves through five phases, each building on the last, until your message and your delivery are one.

---

## THE 5 PHASES

**Tag:** The 5-Phase Journey

**Headline:**
From Discovering Your Story to Leading With It.

---

**Phase 01 — Discover**
_The Defining Moment_
Before any message can be crafted, the truth must be found. In this phase, Joanna’s interview-based process uncovers the defining moment behind your work — the experience that shaped your leadership, your values, and your vision. Most leaders have never named it. This is where it surfaces.

**Phase 02 — Clarify**
_Your Core Message_
From your story, we build your message. Not a tagline. Not an elevator pitch. A clear, resonant expression of who you are, what you stand for, and why it matters — articulated in language that is authentically yours and unmistakably powerful.

**Phase 03 — Craft**
_Your Signature Talk or Narrative_
With your message clear, we build the structure. Whether it’s a keynote, a leadership narrative, or a brand story, this phase gives shape to your message — emotional arc, strategic structure, and the stories that bring it to life.

**Phase 04 — Deliver**
_Voice, Presence & Authority_
A message is only as powerful as its delivery. In this phase, we train your body, your voice, and your presence — so that when you speak, the message lands not just intellectually but emotionally. This is where authority becomes embodied.

**Phase 05 — Lead**
_Thought Leadership & Lasting Impact_
The final phase is about integration and amplification. You step fully into your role as a thought leader — speaking in rooms, on stages, in media — with a message that is clear, consistent, and creates lasting influence.

## FAQ QUESTIONS & ANSWERS

**Q: Who is the True Influence Method™️ designed for?**
A: Joanna works primarily with founders, CEOs, executives, and mission-driven leaders who have built something meaningful and are ready to articulate it — to rooms, boards, media, and the world. Her Speak & Rise group program welcomes a broader range of emerging and established leaders. Her corporate programs serve leadership teams at every level.

---

**Q: What makes this different from traditional speaking coaching?**
A: Most coaching starts with the speech. Joanna starts with you. Through her signature interview-based process, she surfaces the truth behind your work — the defining moment, the real message, the lived experience that makes a leader worth following. What you get is not a polished performance. It is your actual voice.

---

**Q: I’m not sure which program is right for me. How do I decide?**
A: The best first step is completing Joanna’s private client inquiry form. Once she and her team have a sense of your goals, your timeline, and where you are in your leadership journey, they will guide you to the right path. There is no pressure — just a conversation.

---

**Q: What does Private Training involve, and how long does it take?**
A: Private Training is a 90-day engagement with Joanna, structured around 4 to 14 sessions depending on the level you choose (Beginning, Intermediate, or Advanced). During that time, you also join Joanna’s quarterly Mastermind & Retreat. Most clients describe it as the most transformative professional work they’ve ever done.

---

**Q: What is Speak & Rise and who is it for?**
A: Speak & Rise is Joanna’s group training program — the most accessible entry into the True Influence Method™️. It includes live sessions with Joanna, story development work, peer community, and access to The Vault. The 90-Day Mastermind & Retreat starts from $6,000. It is ideal for leaders who are ready to develop their voice and want to do so within a community.

---

**Q: Do you work with companies and organizations?**
A: Yes. Joanna offers fully customized corporate programs including workshops, executive communication intensives, and the True Influence License — for organizations who want to embed the True Influence Method™️ in-house with their own facilitators. Pricing is custom based on scope and team size.

---

**Q: What results can I expect?**
A: Every client journey is different, but common outcomes include: a clearly articulated leadership message, a signature talk or narrative, increased confidence speaking in high-stakes settings, stronger presence in rooms and on stages, and a deeper sense of ownership over their story and impact. Many clients describe a shift that extends far beyond communication — into how they lead, how they’re seen, and what they believe is possible.

---

**Q: How do I get started?**
A: Fill out the private client inquiry form. Joanna’s team will review your submission and reach out to schedule a consultation. From there, you’ll have everything you need to decide on the right path forward.
